NO.2

    We are dedicated to improving our daily dietary habits to create an eco-friendly environment.
    Users can upload photos or images of their meals to the online platform, where AI image recognition provides information about the food, including methane emissions, calories, constipation risk, and electricity generation values. This allows users to engage more deeply with information related to both personal health and environmental issues.
    Additionally, through interactive gaming, users can daily upload relevant images for AI recognition. The recognized information transforms into various elements, and by collecting these elements across different categories, users evolve and receive diverse feedback in the game. This design not only enhances users' understanding of the nutritional values of their daily food intake but also fosters adaptability to personal environmental and energy issues through immersion in gaming interaction.
